VENUS


Equatorial diameter 12 104 KM
Mass 4.9 x 10^24 KG
Mean Distance from sun 108 M KM
Rotation period 243 Days
Solar orbit period 225 Days
Surface gravity 8.9 M/S^2
Surface temperature 462 C
Although venus has very similar size and interior structure as earth , its vulcanic surface and extremely hot and dense atmosohere makes it one of the most inhospitable places in the solar system .

ATOMSPHERE
Thick clouds of sulfuric acid and dust reflect most of the sun's heat inside the atmosphere , causing a runaway greenhouse effect . The atmosphere pressure on the surface of venus is 92 times greater than on earth .
