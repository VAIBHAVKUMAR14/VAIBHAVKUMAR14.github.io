Mars is the fourth planet from the sun and the second smallest planet in the solar system . The reddish apperance of mars' surface is caused by iron oxide (rust).

SURFACE AND MOONS
Mars has a dramatic landscape , with towering volcanoes and a great canyon system of all the planets in the solar system. Mars has two small natural satelites , phobos and deimos , thar orbit very close to the planet . These moons may be captured asteroids. it has been predicted that in about 50 millons years , phobos will either crash into Mars's surface or break up into a ring structure around the planet .
